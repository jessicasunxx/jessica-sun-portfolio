PCB Pendant

A wearable PCB pendant featuring a TL555 timer chip that creates a flashing LED pattern, powered by a 3.7V coin cell battery. This project explores PCB design, fabrication, and the integration of electronics into wearable form.

Materials

  • TL555 timer chip (3.7V compatible)
  • LED (appropriate for 3.7V operation)
  • Resistors and capacitors for timing circuit
  • 3.7V coin cell battery
  • Battery holder
  • PCB substrate (milled on CNC)
  • Soldering equipment

Process

  • Designed PCB layout using PCB design software, creating a pendant form that accommodates the 555 timer circuit.
  • Milled the PCB on the lab CNC machine, creating traces and component pads.
  • Soldered components onto the board, starting with the TL555 chip and supporting passive components.
  • Integrated the LED and battery holder, ensuring proper polarity and connections.
  • Tested the flashing pattern and adjusted timing components as needed.
  • Added attachment mechanism (chain, cord, or pin) to make it wearable.
